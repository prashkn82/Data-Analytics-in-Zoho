# KPI Definitions

## 1. Overview
This document defines all Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used across Zoho Analytics dashboards.  
These KPIs provide unified visibility into Sales, Finance, Support, Customer 360, and Product Performance.  
Each KPI includes its formula, purpose, data source, and usage notes.

---

## 2. Sales KPIs

### **2.1 Total Sales**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Deals.Amount)

**Purpose:**  
Measures total revenue generated from closed deals.

**Data Source:**  
Deals table

---

### **2.2 Win Rate**
**Formula:**  
(Won Deals / Total Deals) × 100

**Purpose:**  
Indicates sales effectiveness and pipeline conversion.

**Data Source:**  
Deals table (Stage field)

---

### **2.3 Average Deal Size**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Deals.Amount) / COUNT(Deals.Deal_ID)

**Purpose:**  
Shows average revenue per deal.

**Data Source:**  
Deals table

---

### **2.4 Pipeline Value**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Deals.Amount WHERE Stage != 'Closed Won')

**Purpose:**  
Represents potential future revenue.

**Data Source:**  
Deals table

---

### **2.5 Sales by Month**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Deals.Amount) GROUP BY MONTH(Closing_Date)

**Purpose:**  
Identifies monthly sales trends.

**Data Source:**  
Deals table

---

## 3. Finance KPIs

### **3.1 Total Invoices**
**Formula:**  
COUNT(Invoices.Invoice_ID)

**Purpose:**  
Tracks invoice volume.

**Data Source:**  
Invoices table

---

### **3.2 Total Payments**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Payments.Amount)

**Purpose:**  
Measures total collected revenue.

**Data Source:**  
Payments table

---

### **3.3 Outstanding Amount**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Invoices.Total) − SUM(Payments.Amount)

**Purpose:**  
Shows pending receivables.

**Data Source:**  
Invoices, Payments

---

### **3.4 Tax Summary**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Invoices.Tax_Amount)

**Purpose:**  
Tracks total tax collected.

**Data Source:**  
Invoices table

---

### **3.5 Revenue by Category**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Invoices.Total) GROUP BY Product.Category

**Purpose:**  
Shows revenue contribution by product category.

**Data Source:**  
Invoices, Products

---

## 4. Support KPIs

### **4.1 Total Tickets**
**Formula:**  
COUNT(Tickets.Ticket_ID)

**Purpose:**  
Measures support workload.

**Data Source:**  
Tickets table

---

### **4.2 Average Resolution Time**
**Formula:**  
AVG(Tickets.Closed_Time − Tickets.Created_Time)

**Purpose:**  
Evaluates support efficiency.

**Data Source:**  
Tickets table

---

### **4.3 SLA Breaches**
**Formula:**  
COUNT(Tickets WHERE Resolution_Time > SLA_Limit)

**Purpose:**  
Tracks SLA compliance.

**Data Source:**  
Tickets, SLA table

---

### **4.4 Tickets by Status**
**Formula:**  
COUNT(Tickets) GROUP BY Status

**Purpose:**  
Shows ticket distribution.

**Data Source:**  
Tickets table

---

### **4.5 Agent Performance**
**Formula:**  
COUNT(Tickets WHERE Agent = X)

**Purpose:**  
Measures workload and productivity per agent.

**Data Source:**  
Tickets, Agents

---

## 5. Customer 360 KPIs

### **5.1 Lifetime Value (LTV)**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Invoices.Total) WHERE Customer_ID = X

**Purpose:**  
Shows total revenue generated by a customer.

**Data Source:**  
Invoices table

---

### **5.2 Customer Support Load**
**Formula:**  
COUNT(Tickets WHERE Customer_ID = X)

**Purpose:**  
Measures customer support demand.

**Data Source:**  
Tickets table

---

### **5.3 Customer Revenue Trend**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Invoices.Total) GROUP BY MONTH(Invoice_Date)

**Purpose:**  
Shows revenue trend per customer.

**Data Source:**  
Invoices table

---

### **5.4 Customer Category Distribution**
**Formula:**  
COUNT(Customers) GROUP BY Category

**Purpose:**  
Shows customer segmentation.

**Data Source:**  
Accounts table

---

## 6. Product Performance KPIs

### **6.1 Total Product Revenue**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Order_Items.Quantity × Order_Items.Unit_Price)

**Purpose:**  
Measures total revenue per product.

**Data Source:**  
Order Items, Products

---

### **6.2 Total Units Sold**
**Formula:**  
SUM(Order_Items.Quantity)

**Purpose:**  
Shows product sales volume.

**Data Source:**  
Order Items

---

### **6.3 Category Contribution**
**Formula:**  
(SUM(Revenue per Category) / Total Revenue) × 100

**Purpose:**  
Shows category-level revenue contribution.

**Data Source:**  
Order Items, Products

---

### **6.4 Top Products**
**Formula:**  
ORDER BY SUM(Revenue) DESC LIMIT 10

**Purpose:**  
Identifies best-performing products.

**Data Source:**  
Order Items

---

## 7. KPI Validation Rules

### **7.1 Data Accuracy**
- Validate totals against reconciliation report  
- Validate counts against CRM/Books/Desk  
- Validate date formats  

### **7.2 Functional Checks**
- Filter behavior  
- Drill-down functionality  
- Cross-module linking  

### **7.3 Performance Checks**
- Query optimization  
- Dashboard load time  
- Cache refresh validation  

---

## 8. Conclusion
These KPI definitions provide a unified measurement framework across Sales, Finance, Support, Customer 360, and Product Performance dashboards.  
They ensure consistent reporting, accurate analytics, and reliable business insights across Zoho Analytics.
