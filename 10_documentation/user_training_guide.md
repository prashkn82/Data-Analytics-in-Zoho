User Training Guide — Zoho CRM, Books, Desk & Analytics

Version: 1.0Last Updated: 2026-07-05Author: Data Engineering / Migration Team

1. Purpose

This guide trains end‑users on how to navigate, use, and maintain the modules migrated into Zoho CRM, Books, Desk, and Analytics. It is designed for business users, support teams, and administrators.

2. Audience

Sales teams (CRM)

Finance teams (Books)

Support teams (Desk)

Management & analysts (Analytics)

Admins are responsible for data maintenance

3. Training Modules Overview

Modules Covered:

Zoho CRM (Leads, Contacts, Accounts)

Zoho Books (Items, Invoices)

Zoho Desk (Tickets)

Zoho Analytics (Dashboards & KPIs)

4. Zoho CRM — User Training

4.1 Logging In

Use company credentials

MFA may be required

4.2 Navigating CRM Modules

Leads: New prospects

Contacts: Individuals associated with accounts

Accounts: Companies/organizations

4.3 Creating a Lead

Go to Leads module

Click Create Lead

Fill mandatory fields: Lead Name, Email

Save

4.4 Converting Leads to Contacts/Accounts

Open a lead → Click Convert

CRM auto‑creates Contact + Account

4.5 Searching & Filtering Records

Use global search

Apply filters (Country, Status, Owner)

4.6 Editing & Updating Records

Click record → Edit

Update fields → Save

4.7 Importing Data (Admin)

Go to Setup → Data Administration → Import

Upload CSV

Map fields

Validate import logs

5. Zoho Books — User Training

5.1 Items Module

View product catalogue

Edit item details (price, category)

5.2 Creating an Invoice

Go to Sales → Invoices

Click New Invoice

Select customer

Add items

Save & send

5.3 Payments

Record payments

View outstanding invoices

5.4 Importing Items/Invoices (Admin)

Upload CSV templates

Validate import logs

6. Zoho Desk — User Training

6.1 Ticket Management

View incoming tickets

Assign to agents

Update status (Open, In Progress, Closed)

6.2 Responding to Tickets

Use the reply editor

Add notes

Attach files

6.3 Ticket Automation

Auto‑assignment rules

SLA tracking

6.4 Importing Tickets (Admin)

Upload CSV

Map fields

Validate import logs

7. Zoho Analytics — User Training

7.1 Dashboard Navigation

Access dashboards via Analytics Home

Modules:

Customer Master

Sales

Inventory

Migration Reconciliation

7.2 Filtering Data

Use filters (Date, Region, Category)

Drill‑down into charts

7.3 Exporting Reports

Export to PDF, Excel

Schedule automated email reports

7.4 Creating Custom Reports (Advanced Users)

Select dataset

Choose chart type

Add metrics

Save & publish

8. Best Practices

Keep CRM/Books data updated

Use consistent naming conventions

Validate data before imports

Review dashboards weekly

Follow SLA guidelines in Desk
